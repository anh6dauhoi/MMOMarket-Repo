Complaint Policy

COMPLAINT POLICY - MMO MARKET

ARTICLE 1: GENERAL PRINCIPLES

1.1. Good Faith Principle: All complaints must be made honestly, in good faith, and based on actual issues arising from the transaction.

1.2. Complaint Deadline: The deadline varies by product category:

  • Warning Category Products (Game keys, codes, giftcards): Complaint deadline is 3 days from successful transaction. This is the escrow period during which the system holds Coins.
  • Common Category Products (Educational accounts, streaming accounts with validity period): Complaint deadline is from successful transaction until product expiration as described.
  • Note: After 3 days for Warning products, Coins are transferred to the Seller. Complaints after this period will be supported based on Seller's reputation and evidence provided, but 100% refund is not guaranteed.

1.3. Resolution Process: Complaints follow a two-step process:

  1. Buyer resolves with Seller directly
  2. Request Admin intervention if parties cannot reach agreement

ARTICLE 2: MANDATORY EVIDENCE REQUIREMENTS

This is the most important clause. A valid complaint MUST include corresponding evidence based on product type.

2.1. For Warning Category Products:

  • Applicable products: Game keys, software keys, codes, giftcards, high-value game/software accounts, or digital products that can only be activated once.
  • MANDATORY evidence requirement: Buyer must provide ONE continuous video, without cuts/pauses or screen obscuration, recording the entire usage process from start.
  • Video content must clearly show:
    1. Start recording from when you receive product information on MMO Market transaction detail page and activate the product
    2. Clearly show copying (copy) product information (key, account, password...)
    3. Clearly show switching screen to activation platform (e.g., Steam, Epic Games, software website...)
    4. Clearly show pasting (paste) or entering product information into that platform
    5. Clearly show the result and error message displayed on the activation platform screen
  • Reason: This is the only way to prove the product code/account was defective from the start and has not been used by the Buyer.

2.2. For Common Category Products:

  • Applicable products: Common accounts (education, streaming), documents, tools, and other digital products not in Warning Category.
  • MANDATORY evidence requirement: Buyer must provide valid evidence in video OR clear image format, without editing, proving the issue encountered.
  • Valid evidence examples:
    • Screenshot showing login error (e.g., "Wrong password", "Account does not exist")
    • Short video recording login process and unsuccessful result
    • Screenshot showing account features not matching Seller's description (e.g., downgraded plan)

ARTICLE 3: CASES NOT SUPPORTED FOR RESOLUTION

MMO Market will refuse to process complaints if they fall into one of the following cases:

  • 3.1. Not providing evidence or evidence not valid according to Article 2 regulations
  • 3.2. Complaint does not comply with deadline specified in Article 1.2 for each product category
  • 3.3. Error caused by Buyer (e.g., self-modifying account information and losing access, violating third-party platform policy leading to account lock)
  • 3.4. Buyer complaining about product features not described or committed by Seller
  • 3.5. Cases of changing mind, no longer wanting to buy product after transaction completed
